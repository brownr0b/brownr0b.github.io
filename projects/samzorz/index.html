About Me

Hey! My name is Sam Hall.

More commonly referred to as Samzorz here on the interwebs, I was born and raised in Phoenix, Arizona and now currently reside in Chandler, Arizona. I am a full-time broadcaster on TwitchTV, where I have just celebrated my five year anniversary. At 25 years old, I never thought I would be playing video games for a living, but here I am living the dream!

I used to compete at the highest level possible for Call of Duty, but have since retired and created the amazing community on Twitch known as the Sampires.
